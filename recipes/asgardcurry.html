Asgard curry

Description:

Summon the flavors of the gods with Asgard Curry — a bold and hearty dish worthy of a Viking feast. Infused with warming spices, tender meat, and rich, golden sauce, this legendary curry will conquer your hunger!


Ingredients:

  • 1 kg diced lamb or beef (or chicken if preferred) – the heart of the beast
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil – for searing
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped – eyes of the all-seeing ravens
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced – the breath of the gods
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of fresh ginger, grated – fire from the forge
  • 2 tbsp curry powder – the golden dust of far-off realms
  • Fresh coriander or parsley, chopped – green of Yggdrasil
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste...

Forging the Asgard Curry:

  1. Sear the Meat
    2. Heat oil in a large iron pot. Brown the meat on all sides over high heat — seal in the strength of the beast. Remove and set aside.
  2. Build the Base
    3. In the same pot, lower the heat. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Cook until soft and golden — this is the flavor hearth.
  3. Add the Spices
    4. Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and chili flakes. Let the spices awaken for 1–2 minutes, until their aroma fills the hall.
  4. Summon the Sauce
    5. Return the meat to the pot. Pour in chopped tomatoes and coconut milk. Stir well, season with salt and pepper, then let it simmer on low heat for 45–60 minutes — until the meat is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Feast Like a Viking
    6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot over rice or with flatbread. Garnish with fresh herbs and eat like the gods of Asgard.
